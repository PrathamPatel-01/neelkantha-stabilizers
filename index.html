About Neelkantha Stabilizers

29 Years of
Excellence

Founded in 1995, Neelkantha Stabilizers has been at the forefront of voltage stabilization technology, delivering reliable power solutions to homes, businesses, and industrial facilities across India.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility combines traditional craftsmanship with modern engineering to produce stabilizers that meet the highest quality standards. Every unit undergoes rigorous testing to ensure peak performance even in the most demanding electrical environments.

  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified Manufacturing
  • BIS Marked Products for Quality Assurance
  • Pan-India Service Network
  • 24/7 Technical Support

Our Services

End-to-end power solutions from consultation and installation to maintenance and repair.

Installation & Commissioning

Our certified technicians handle complete on-site installation and commissioning of stabilizers, ensuring correct wiring, earthing, and load balancing.

  • Site survey and load assessment
  • Professional wiring and earthing
  • System commissioning and testing

Annual Maintenance Contracts

Keep your stabilizers running at peak efficiency with our comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts covering routine servicing and emergency support.

  • Scheduled preventive maintenance
  • Priority response for breakdowns
  • Genuine spare parts guarantee

Diagnostics & Repair

Advanced diagnostics to pinpoint faults fast. We repair all makes and models of voltage stabilizers with a quick turnaround time.

  • On-site and in-workshop repair
  • PCB-level fault diagnosis
  • Transformer rewinding

Power Quality Consulting

Expert power quality analysis to identify voltage fluctuations, harmonic distortions, and load imbalances affecting your operations.

  • Customised stabilizer recommendations
  • Energy saving analysis
